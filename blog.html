CHRIST IS OUR PEACE

Posted by Joseph Samuel King in Word of the day Views 33

Christ Greetings Our Heavenly Family.
HAPPY ETERNITY! RULE FOREVER!!!

Now, LET'S TALK CHRIST; specifically, let us indulge in the very important subject of Christ as our Peace. Two Texts from Scripture serve to provide the starting reference for what the Spirit is saying here:
Isaiah 9:6 (KJV) For unto us a child is born, unto us a son is given: and the government shall be upon his shoulder: and his name shall be called Wonderful, Counsellor, The mighty God, The everlasting Father, The Prince of Peace.
And,
John 14:27 (KJV)
Peace I leave with you, my peace I give unto you: not as the world giveth, give I unto you. Let not your heart be troubled, neither let it be afraid.
We are living in a very disturbed world plagued presently with wars, rumors of wars, and marked divisions. These trials are seen in nearly every continent and are so significant in some cases, even threatening world wars like in Europe with the Russian invasion of Ukraine, or in the case of China's threat to invade Taiwan. Here in Kenya where our ministry is based, a very polarising election has concluded in deep disagreements and accusations, and again there are those that fear that dissatisfied losers will fight it out, if not in court, in the streets.
The world desperately needs peace. And it appears that even the very ideals and institutions that many have embraced and believed in to ensure peace are in fact, sponsoring wars and divisions.

Democracy is such an ideal ocassioning marked strife in the United States of America where it's reputedly to have been best practiced. There we see a country irreparably divided following their last presidential elections in 2020. Kenya is not alone in this; in Africa, it is joined by nearly every other country after an election season. Consequently, many are despairing for peace. Many are calling for calm and hoping for agreeable resolutions in the face of the marked divides.
Reality is that God is indeed shaking the Heavens and the Earth, and He is bringing men around the world to reckon His Son as the only Guarantor for PEACE. Where men have trusted in their ideals and institutions, their own means of realising peace, God is shaking them up to bring them to reckon Christ Jesus as their PEACE.
Beloveds, it is essential that you wake up to Christ Jesus as your PEACE. Democracy cannot guarantee peace, elections cannot guarantee peace, the governments of men cannot guarantee peace, not even the wishes of men can guarantee peace; the only Way, the only Truth, the only Life, that guarantees PEACE is that of Christ Jesus, the Prince of Peace. Indeed, when the Prophet Isaiah heralded His Birth, He was prospecting with joy the birth of the only guarantee of Peace on Earth, the Prince of Peace.
Saints of God, Christ Jesus is our Peace; and not just for the world but even for you personally. In a world plagued by strife, by disagreements, hatred, wars, anger, even fear, you can rise above all that and have peace, peace that surpasses understanding (Philippians 4:7). And not only that, you can be so peaceful, so filled with peace that you become an ambassador of peace, God's Ambassador sharing His Peace in this world riddled with troubles (2 Corinthians 5:19, 20).
This is partly the reason why Apostle Sarah and I are sharing LET'S TALK CHRIST with you, why as a daily devotional that will feature on our website- @www.apostlejosephandsarah.co.ke- we will communicate daily with you, that you should know Christ, the Prince of Peace, and walk in His Peace, even grow in His Word to become His Ambassador of Peace. It is our deepest desire that you will join us in bearing the gospel of peace in this world plagued by wars:
Romans 10:15 (KJV)
And how shall they preach, except they be sent? as it is written, How beautiful are the feet of them that preach the gospel of peace, and bring glad tidings of good things!
Let us PRAY:
Father God,
You are our God, the Great I Am
to Whom we look for everything.
We receive Christ Jesus as our Peace.
Help us embrace Him and be anchored in His Power that we should have His Peace.
Thank You for doing so in Jesus' Name.
AMEN and AMEN!!!

SHALOM!
Your Servant in the Grace of Christ Jesus

Joseph Samuel King
An Apostle of Christ Jesus
