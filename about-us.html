Who We Are

We are committed to being and making disciples. Disciples are people who are so in love with Jesus, they have decided that the most important thing in their lives is to put Him first. As Christians, we become disciples, striving to live according to the teachings of Jesus. Part of being a disciple of Jesus is making disciples.
Apostle Joseph Samuel King and Apostle Sarah Keiza King have a heavenly assignment which is enormous in every way yet the grace of Christ Jesus abides in them sufficiently enough to ensure that they accomplish it in glory and power.

About Our Mission

Revelation Christ Ministries is a multifaceted work of God presided over by Apostle Joseph Samuel King, who is duly assisted by his wife, Apostle Sarah Keiza King; both of whom carry an exceptional anointing specially assigned by Christ Jesus to share His Heavenly Victory, establish His Reign in the Earth over all the creation.

What We Believe

Be completely humble and gentle, be patient, bearing with one another in love. Make every effort to keep the unity of the Spirit through the bond of peace. one God and Father of all, who is over all and through all and in all. But to each one of us grace has been given as Christ apportioned it.

Looking for a church?

We would love to have you join our ministry and become part of us. Visit us today and commune with us. Let us be your family as we become yours as well. We would be honoured to have you be part of us.

Service to Others

We have services that we provide to the less fortunate as well as our own. Our outreach programs are based on our groups. We have groups from the Men's Group, Women's group, Outreach group and different cell groups.

FREE MINISTRY, NO CHARGE

Contrary to many in this generation that sell the divine workings and gifting of the Spirit, Apostles Joseph and Sarah King have humbly obeyed the Christ never to sell His Commission or any of His Gifts; ensuring that the Word of God is sent and not sold, the Gifts of the Spirit are shared and not hired (Matthew 10:8). In doing so, they have been enabled by God through the kind and generous donations of their partners and friends (@God’s Helping Hearts and Hands) so that everything they do is truly for Christ, for His Glory, and the benefit of His Bride. Like Apostle Joseph is won’t to say, emphatically- ‘The Blood of Christ Jesus has fully paid for every spiritual blessing pertaining to life and godliness, including also, and especially for, the Things that pertain to Christ like His Word and Power’. This website is an open door invitation to you, to everyone, to come and dine in the Lord’s Heavenly Banquet.

FREE GOSPEL BOOKS AND MUSIC

Consequently, you will find easy access to amazing books authored by Apostle Joseph King that have indeed blessed people around the world like- Winner, Don’t Quit, The Discipline of The Anointing, The Amazing Case For Grace, Change You Must Believe In, The Value of A Name, and Go For It. You will also get his music without any charge (both videos and audios) and be lifted up in praise and worship with such moving songs like- The Anointing Falls On Me, Peace on Earth, Praying For My Wife, You Are Holy, Cheza Party (Enjoy the Party), and many more. And given the regularity with which the Spirit quickens him so that he is enabled to write more and more music and books, this website is the place where you will be kept abreast with whatever is new from this inspiring vessel of the Most High.

LET’S TALK CHRIST DEVOTIONAL

Indeed, the site is Heavenly Loaded and Divinely Rich with much more, including the Let’s Talk Christ Daily Devotional (also FREE) which will feature daily write-ups centered on the Doctrine of Christ Jesus, His Gospel of the Kingdom of Heaven, and properly presented to suit contemporary needs. @Let’s Talk Christ, Apostles Joseph and Sarah King, delve into various concerns and interests in the Church and the world today, addressing them as the Spirit guides them, enabling their articulation to ensure that those devoted to following them are enlightened in the Gospel, disciple in the Knowledge of Christ, and equipped for good ministration. It is in many ways a vital tool for inspiration and discipleship guaranteed to build ones spirit for divine action.Get there every day and you can be sure to have days of God’s Word shoring you above today’s tests and temptations. Let’s Talk Christ is therefore more than a daily devotional, it is also a daily armoring of believers with the Sword of the Spirit, a daily charging of their spirit with God’s Counsel to ensure their continuous victory in Christ (2 Corinthians 2:14). The daily write-ups will be complimented with a bi-weekly Let’s Talk Christ online broadcast show that features Apostles Joseph and Sarah King sharing on the prevailing themes the daily write-ups are focused on.

HEAVENLY FRIDAY’S HOLY COMMUNION SERVICE

This website also provides opportunity for Apostles Joseph and Sarah King to invite you to a profound and regular sharing of the Lord’s Holy Table in their flagship program- Heavenly Friday Holy Communion Service. A once-every week broadcast show that addresses the serious need in the Body of Christ where so many spend very long durations before they celebrate the Body and the Blood of our Lord as He indeed required that we do in remembrance of Him (Luke 22:14-23). The show features sound doctrinal teachings on the Heavenly Communion that Christ Jesus brought us into as a means of enlightening the minds of saints regarding its rich meanings as well as a force of reviving its devotion and practice by believers everywhere. Every broadcast features that solemn and divine moment when the believer will be brought to reflect and bear on the seriousness of the Heavenly Rite of eating the Body and drinking the Blood of our Lord. A continuous attendance of the broadcast guarantees to strengthen the viewer in the Faith and Works of Christ Jesus ensuring that they are fruitful in every good work.

LOVE THAT REALLY WORKS

This broadcast interview program is inspired by Apostle Joseph’s inspiring book based on the same title and it features both him and Apostle Sarah. Together they touch several foundational aspects on love in its all-encompassing interaction- between spouses, lovers, friends, colleagues, associates, in the ministry, among the brethren, in the community, even for strangers. Apostles Joseph and Sarah are particularly inspired to share on love given the fact of its central significance in the life of faith; for love is indeed not only the full sum of the law but even more importantly, the very essence of the life of Christ (Galatians 5:6, 14)Apostles Joseph and Sarah also believe that both the church and the world as it are in a desperate need for love. Its shortage is occasioning divisions and strife that are threatening the very existence of faith and life on Earth. They believe that God has specifically appointed them to champion a revival of love centered on the Heavenly Affection God demonstrated for all men on the Cross and by the Blood of His Son Christ Jesus.
